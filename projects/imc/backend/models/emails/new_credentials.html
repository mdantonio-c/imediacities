I-Media-Cities is an ambitious and innovative research project led by 9 European cultural institutions (film and audiovisual archives from 8 countries), to share, provide access to, and use their digital content, turning it into the lever of novel approaches to multidisciplinary research, to business innovation and to the overall accessibility of the European cultural heritage. As an experimental innovation action, the project focuses on the digital content that refers to cities.

Over the past months we created a first test version of our online platform which will guide you as an user through all the available content.

You as a researcher or an archivist are key to the success of this platform and therefore we would like to ask 30 minutes of your precious time to try out the platform and provide us with valuable feedback which will empower us to make further improvements.
